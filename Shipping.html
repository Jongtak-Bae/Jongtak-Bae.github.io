Shipping

All orders will be shipped within 1-3 days. They can take anywhere from 3-15 days (depending on the destination) to deliver.
Please note, this means orders made outside Canada may take longer.

Express orders placed after 5pm EST will be shipped on the next business day. Please note, Express delivery is only available within Canada.

Once your order ships, you will recieve a Shipment Confirmaiton email with a tracking number. If orders are split across multiple shipments,
you will receive multiple tracking numbers.


ORDERS WITHIN CANADA

  • Orders that are $80 CAD or more qualify for FREE SHIPPING
  • We charge a flat rate of $8 on orders less than $80 CAD
  • Upgrading to Experss shipping may result in additional charges and will vary depending on location
  • Fees will be calculated at checkout

ORDERS SHIPPING TO THE USA

  • Orders to the US that are $100 CAD or more qualify for FREE SHIPPING
  • We charge a flat rate of $12 CAD on orders less than $100 CAD to the US
  • Upgrading to Experss shipping may result in additional charges and will vary
    depending on location
  • Fees will be calculated at checkout

INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

  • If you are ordering from outside Canada or the US, the shipping fee will be calculated at checkout
  • Fees will be determined based on your location, the weight, and the dimensions of the package
  • Please note, some products may not ship to all locations. If this is the case, it will be in the product description
  • Fees will be calculated at checkout
Returns

We’re sure you’ll absolutely LOVE your purchase, however if you are not completely satisfied with an item, you may return it to us for a full refund less any shipping charges. We will accept items for return within 30 days of the order shipment date. Sadly we are unable to accept return requests made more than 30 days after the original ship date. Please note, Clearance items are FINAL SALE and connot be returned.

Returned items must be delivered to us unsued, in the original packaging (if possible), and in the condition they were received in or they may not be eligible for refund. Please allow 1-2 weeks for your refund to be processed.

To request a return, please fill out the form bellow and we will process it as soon as we can.
You may also email us at help@surprises.com or call us at (416) 495-8428 if you require additional assistance.
Be sure to include your order number in the communication.
